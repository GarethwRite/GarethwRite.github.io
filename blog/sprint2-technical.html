whats the differrence between Margin, Border and padding?

The key difference is the margin deals with the space between the border and the next element, while the padding is the space inside the element between the element and the border. So that means the border is between the padding and the margin.

Put simply or my analogy

Think of a present, where the element is the present and the element being anyhting from a p(paragraph) to a section, so you must protect the present first so there is padding, then wrapped in a border and think of the margin as the ribbon with the fancy string.
