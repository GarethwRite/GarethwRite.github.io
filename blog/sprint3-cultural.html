Emotional intelligence

Emotional intelligence EI or EQ(Emotional quotient) is learning to recognize and control emotions but not only of yourself but also the emotions of others, that may sound abit mind control Dr evil ish but it is meant as in having a high EI give you the ability to harness emotions and act accordingly to them, this helps in overcoming challenges, pushing towards goals and having clarity through listening and observing your own and others emotions. people with a high EI have better mental health, work proformance and better leadership skills.
