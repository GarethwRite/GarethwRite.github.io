What are three new things you have learned about yourself and your ego as a result of the core learning?

I learnt that i dont know as much about coding concepts as i thought, or i had forgotten alot between coding full time at uni, so without regularly coding its easy to lose those skills

I learnt through core that understanding when im stressed or stuck i am the least effective at coding, its like a wall i cant cross until i calm down and clear the mind to solve the problem.

I learnt i had a few core strengths that are inherent with my personality, like growth mindset and planning

What are the role of values, empathy and self awareness in learning and programming?

In programming they are very important, because coding is continous problem solving its highly effective to have awareness of yourself to monitor, so you know if your tired stressed or sad, it gives you the abiltity to change those emotions and use them for good.

What were the most challenging aspects of the core learning?

for me the most challenging thing was acknowledging my weaknesses, as its somthing i tend to avoid, but now i know that acknowledging strengths and weaknesses is a good thing because then you can make changes or adapt to suit yourself.

Why do you think we, a programming school, are spending so much time focusing on core learning in a web development bootcamp course?

I think you do it because it sets us students up with great a foundation to build and learn ourselves as well as being able to work with others effectively, which will not only help us in solving problems/ being able to think, but also help us in the real working world as developers.

Does the time you spent studying core learning here feel like a waste of time? Should you have just used that time to practicing programming instead? Justify your answer

No i dont think so, because we could learn how to code until the cows come home but there will be times when were stuck and confused and irratated but the core skills we learn i think will counter that and in turn will make us better programmers/ developers.
