Growth mindset

what is it and why is it relavant?

A growth mindset is when people think that their abilities basic or new can be developed through dedication and hardwork, this idea creates strong will and determination to push towards goals and makes the individual feel full control in their life, its relavance is simple, its relavant because it creates happier stronger people more willing to try harder obstacles in life to achieve

what the individual wants out of life.?

during this exploratiion nothing really surprised me, I believe that i do have a good Growth mindset, i would almost take a growth mindset to be a positive attitude in life. what would surprise me is if only a minority of people take on the growth mindset atitude, im sure its inherent for some and learned by others but the world would be a lovely place if everyone had a growth mindset.
